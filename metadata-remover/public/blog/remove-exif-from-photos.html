What EXIF Contains (and Why It Matters)

  • GPS coordinates: precise location the photo was taken
  • Camera info: make, model, serial
  • Capture settings: aperture, shutter speed, ISO
  • Timestamps: date/time of capture and edits
  • Thumbnails & color profiles: hidden data blocks

Does Removing EXIF Affect Image Quality?

No. EXIF is separate from the pixel data. Removing it doesn’t compress or blur your image.

Mobile & Social Tips

  • iPhone: Some share-sheets strip location but keep other EXIF. Use a dedicated remover for certainty.
  • Android: Manufacturer galleries vary. Use a remover for consistent results.
  • Social apps: Many compress or strip some metadata, but not always all. Clean before uploading.
