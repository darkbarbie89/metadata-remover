What is Metadata?

Metadata is hidden information embedded in your files. Photos contain location data, camera settings, and timestamps. PDFs reveal author names, company info, and editing history. This data can compromise your privacy when sharing files online.

Why Remove Metadata?

  • Protect your location privacy from photos
  • Remove personal information from documents
  • Prevent tracking through file properties
  • Comply with privacy regulations
